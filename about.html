WHO ARE WE?

Etechsino (Shenzhen Etechsino Sci &Tech Co., Ltd) established in 2015, locates at Xuzhou where is the top glass production center in China, we provide a wide variety of glass bottles and glass jars to the food & beverage industries, and with professional R&D team who can customize glass products to meet different customer’s requirement, we sell to more than 20 countries and area in the worldwide.

WHAT CAN WE PROVIDE?

We commit to provide safe, healthy and non-toxic glass container, and our main products: glass bottle, wine bottle, glass jar, jam jar, storage jar, mason jar, juice bottle, milk bottle, oil bottle, beverage bottle, ranging from 15ml to 5000ml. For the hot-selling models, we have 80 types of glass bottle in stock.
